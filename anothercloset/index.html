The Mission

Another Closet aims to save the planet’s resources by helping the Fashion Industry to transition its business model from selling to renting out merchandise.

The Environment

We are passionate about the environmental decimation we see taking place today, and eager to find ways to contribute to improving the situation. Another Closet will help the Fashion Industry transition from a linear to a circular and more sustainable model.

Fashion

We are passionate about fashion and realistic about the trends which are driving the constant pressure on our ecosystem and our world. If we are able to move forward, we will be able to have a sorely-needed impact on a powerful industry, which can change the way we think about fashion, ownership, and the world.

The Team

As a team, Another Closet is diverse not only in terms of professional background, but also in terms of international experience. With professional careers spanning 3 continents and covering the fashion industry, business and tech, we are uniquely positioned to understand and help our customers with their rental challenges.
