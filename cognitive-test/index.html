Your Trolly

Memory Test Experiment
Imagine that you, your best friend, and a stranger have gone grocery shopping together. Once on your way out, you decide to organise all the groceries you bought into the boot of the car. You each have bought 40 items. This computer game is going to display the name of various groceries 1 by 1, and if you have that item in your trolley, you must put it in your car’s boot. If the item belongs to one of the other people (your best friend or the stranger), they will place it into their car’s boot. There is no time limit. Are you ready?
